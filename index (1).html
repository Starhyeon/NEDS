About Our Mission

The National Environmental Data Network is a student-led initiative to collect standardized microplastic data from beaches across the United States. Using NOAA-approved protocols, we're building a comprehensive database that helps researchers, policymakers, and communities understand the scope of plastic pollution in our coastal ecosystems.

Our data is freely available to researchers, government agencies, and environmental organizations working to protect our oceans.
